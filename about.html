HISTORY

The SUNBIRD started on the year 2008 and it is owned by Rameses Alesna and also he is the branch manager of St. Peter Life Plan Tagbilaran branch. At first this farm was located at Binayran Rd., San Isidro District. After two years it was transferred at the San Isidro District proper and in the year 2016 it is transferred again at Dao District. This farm has many kinds of fighting cocks and each of them has a different fighting skills
